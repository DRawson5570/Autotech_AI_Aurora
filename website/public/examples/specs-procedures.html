How do I reset the oil life on a 2020 Chevy Silverado?

2020 Silverado Oil Life Reset:

  1. Turn ignition ON (engine off)
  2. Press accelerator pedal 3x within 5 seconds
  3. "Oil Life Reset" message appears
  4. Turn ignition off, then start engine to verify

Throttle Body Relearn

You ask:

How to do throttle body relearn on 2017 Nissan Altima?

AI responds:

2017 Altima Idle Air Volume Learning:

  1. Warm engine to operating temp, then turn OFF
  2. Wait 10 seconds with key out
  3. Turn key ON (don't start) for 3 seconds
  4. Quickly press gas pedal 5x within 5 seconds
  5. Wait 7 seconds, then press and hold gas pedal 10 sec
  6. Release when CEL blinks
  7. Start engine, let idle for 20 minutes
Window Auto-Up/Down Reset

You ask:

Reset power window auto-up on 2018 Honda Accord

AI responds:

2018 Accord Window Initialization:

  1. Turn ignition to ON
  2. Lower window completely, release switch
  3. Pull UP and hold for 2 seconds after fully closed
  4. Release, then pull UP again and hold 2 seconds
  5. Test auto-up/down function

Repeat for each window that lost memory after battery disconnect

Steering Angle Sensor Reset

You ask:

Steering angle sensor calibration 2016 Toyota Camry

AI responds:

2016 Camry Steering Angle Calibration:

  1. Start engine, let idle
  2. Turn wheel full lock left, hold 2 sec
  3. Turn wheel full lock right, hold 2 sec
  4. Return wheel to center
  5. Drive straight at 15+ mph for 30 seconds

VSC light should turn off. If not, use scan tool to reset via ABS module.

Key Fob Programming

You ask:

Program new key fob to 2014 Dodge Charger

AI responds:

2014 Charger Key Fob Programming:

Requires 2 working fobs to self-program

  1. Enter vehicle with fob #1, close all doors
  2. Press START without brake (accessory mode)
  3. Within 10 sec, hold UNLOCK + PANIC for 2 sec
  4. Release, then press any button on new fob
  5. Chime confirms programming
  6. Press START to exit

If only one fob exists, dealer/locksmith with WITECH required
